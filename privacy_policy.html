Privacy Policy

At Infini Slot we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: such as device model, operating system, and identifiers for advertising (IDFA, GAID).
  • Usage Data: interactions with ads, gameplay statistics, and crash logs.
  • Consent Information: your choices regarding personalized ads through the User Messaging Platform (UMP).

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Serve ads (personalized or non-personalized) via Google AdMob and other networks.
  • Improve app functionality and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including GDPR and CCPA.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Settings
  • Google Ad technology providers

4. Consent & ATT (App Tracking Transparency)

On iOS, you may be asked for permission to allow tracking (ATT). On Android/iOS, you will be shown a consent form via UMP. You may choose personalized or non-personalized ads.

5. Your Choices

You can withdraw or change your ad consent at any time through the app’s privacy settings (if available). You can also reset your device’s advertising identifier in system settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the app or provide personal information.
